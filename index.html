The 3 best tourist sites in the World

The world is filled with a lot of amazing and breath taking sites

  1. The Great Wall of China

    • Location: China
    • No of Visits: 5 million per year

    The Great Wall of China (Chinese: 萬里長城; pinyin: Wànlǐ Chángchéng) is the collective name of a series of fortification systems generally built across the historical northern borders of China to protect and consolidate territories of Chinese states and empires against various nomadic groups of the steppe and their polities.

  2. The Eiffel Tower

    • Location: France
    • No of Visits: 4.5 million per year

    The Eiffel Tower (/ˈaɪfəl/ EYE-fəl; French: tour Eiffel [tuʁ‿ɛfɛl] (About this soundlisten)) is a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. It is named after the engineer Gustave Eiffel, whose company designed and built the tower.

  3. The Burj Khalifa

    • Location: Dubai
    • No of Visits: 4.2 million per year

    Construction of the Burj Khalifa began in 2004, with the exterior completed five years later in 2009. The primary structure is reinforced concrete. The building was opened in 2010 as part of a new development called Downtown Dubai.
